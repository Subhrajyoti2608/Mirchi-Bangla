Mirchi Bangla

Mirchi Bangla is the biggest and most popular radio station of India (Radio Mirchi 98.3 FM). They are popular for there Bengali Audiostory presentation 'Sunday Suspense'.Sunday Suspense is a radio drama (based on horror, detective, suspense stories) with amazing sound scape by DJ Richard, aired on Mirchi 98.3 (Kolkata) and digitally shared by Mirchi Bangla's official YouTube page. The stories are performed by voice-over artistes like Mirchi Mir, Mirchi Deep, Mirchi Ayantika, Mirchi Somak, Mirchi Agni and more. From 2018 they started to make new webseries like OMG, typo by Mirchi Somak & Mirchi Agni, Iti Ayantika & Duni Tunes by Mirchi Ayantika etc.
